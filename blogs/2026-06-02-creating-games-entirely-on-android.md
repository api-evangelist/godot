Godot has released GABE (Godot Android Build Environment), a stable companion app that enables full game development and export on Android devices and XR headsets. Previously, developers needed a PC to perform final exports with Gradle support and plugins; now they can complete the entire workflow from creation through publication directly on mobile devices without requiring a desktop computer.
