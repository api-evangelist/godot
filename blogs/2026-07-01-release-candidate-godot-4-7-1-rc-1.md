This maintenance release addresses critical regressions found since Godot 4.7 launched a week earlier, with 27 contributors landing 41 fixes across animation, GUI, input, navigation, networking, and rendering. Downloads are available for Linux, macOS, and Windows in standard and .NET variants, plus web and XR editors for testing.
